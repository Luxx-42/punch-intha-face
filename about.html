Welcome to The Battle Born Boxing Gym

Our Story

At Battle Born Boxing Gym, we are driven by a single goal: to empower individuals to reach their full potential both in and out of the ring. Founded in 1988, by Rex Dalton, a former professional boxer with a passion for the sport and a vision for a community where everyone can discover their strength and spirit.


Located in the heart of Texas, Battle Born Boxing Gym started as a modest training space but quickly grew into a premier boxing gym known for producing not just skilled boxers but well-rounded individuals. Our name, "Battle Born," reflects our philosophy that every challenge faced in the ring teaches us lessons that are applicable to the battles we face in life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a supportive environment where people from all walks of life can learn the art of boxing, improve their fitness, and build resilience that translates into all aspects of their lives.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the values of boxing such as discipline, respect, and perseverance are embraced in everyday life, helping individuals to tackle any challenge with confidence and integrity.

Our Values

Community: At the heart of Battle Born is a vibrant community of members who support and push each other to excel. Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our training and operations, ensuring that our members receive the best possible experience. Inclusivity: We believe boxing is for everyone and proudly offer programs that cater to both men and women, all ages and skill levels. Integrity: We teach our boxers to conduct themselves with integrity inside and outside of the gym. Resilience: We foster an environment that builds mental and physical resilience, empowering our members to rise above adversity.
