Memberships

Bronze Plan - "Starter Pack"

Price: $59/month
Features:
  • Access to gym facilities during standard hours
  • 4 group classes per month
  • Monthly fitness assessment
  • Access to locker rooms and showers
  • Best for: Beginners looking to get started in boxing or those seeking a basic fitness regime.

Silver Plan - "Fighter's Choice"

Price: $99/month
Features:
  • All benefits of the Bronze Plan
  • Unlimited group classes
  • Weekly personal training sessions
  • Nutrition planning and monitoring
  • Free entry to gym-sponsored competitions
  • 20% discount on merchandise and special events
  • Dedicated locker
  • Best for: Dedicated boxers and athletes focused on competitive boxing or peak physical conditioning.
